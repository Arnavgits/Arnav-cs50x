This page lists some well-known Cricketers

  • Sir Don Bradman: Widely regarded as the greatest cricketer of all time.
  • Sachin Tendulkar: Known as the "Little Master," he holds records for most runs in both Tests and ODIs.
  • Sir Garfield Sobers: One of the most versatile cricketers, excelling in batting, bowling, and fielding.
  • Shane Warne: A legendary leg-spinner, considered one of the best bowlers in the history of the sport.
  • Virat Kohli: Modern-day cricket superstar with numerous records to his name.
