key events and milestones in cricket history in chronological order.

  1. Origins (Pre-1700s): Early forms of cricket, likely originating in England.
  2. Early Formalization (1700-1800): The formalization of cricket, establishment of the first cricket clubs, and the creation of the first international matches.
  3. 19th Century: Expansion of the game globally, first international series, and the birth of Test cricket.
  4. Early 20th Century: The establishment of cricket as a professional sport, iconic players like Don Bradman, and major developments like the introduction of the Ashes series.
